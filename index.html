Welcome to This is Indian Country - Spring 2016

This is Indian Country is a digital cultural map of indigenous community issues worldwide. Collaboratively produced at Michigan State University during the Spring 2016 semester by the students registered in the Contemporary American Indian Communities class (ANP 433), This is Indian Country represents an experiment in applied digital anthropology undergraduate learning.
